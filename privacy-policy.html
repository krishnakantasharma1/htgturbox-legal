📋 Introduction

Welcome to HTG TurboX ("the App"), developed by Hi Tech Gamerz ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our Android application.

By downloading or using HTG TurboX, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you disagree with any part, please discontinue use of the App.

🔒 HTG TurboX is designed with privacy in mind. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

📊 Information We Collect

1. Device Information (Collected Automatically)

  • Device model, manufacturer, and brand (used to show device-specific optimization tips)
  • Android OS version (used to show version-appropriate developer options)
  • RAM usage, storage status, and battery level (read locally, never transmitted)
  • Installed applications list (used only for App Manager and Game Booster features, processed on-device only)

2. Usage Data (via Google AdMob)

  • Advertising ID (used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions, clicks — collected by AdMob)
  • Approximate location (country-level only, used by AdMob for geo-targeted ads)

3. Token Reward Data (Stored Locally)

  • Token balance, daily earn count, and reward history are stored only on your device using Android SharedPreferences
  • This data is never transmitted to our servers

4. Redemption Requests (User-Initiated Only)

  • When you submit a token redemption request, your email app sends us your email address and token balance
  • This information is only used to process your Google Play gift coupon request

🔧 App Permissions Explained

  • INTERNET — Required to load advertisements via Google AdMob and for network speed testing
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Checks internet connectivity before loading ads or running network tests
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — Required to display your installed apps in the App Manager and detect games in Game Booster. No app data leaves your device.
  • ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY — Used for the Do Not Disturb feature in Game Booster, only when you explicitly enable it
  • WAKE_LOCK — Keeps screen on during gaming when "Keep Screen On" is enabled by you
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Sends you optional reminders to clean RAM or boost performance. Can be disabled anytime in Android Settings → Apps → HTG TurboX → Notifications
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Reschedules notification reminders after device restart
  • REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES — Enables the uninstall button in App Manager (opens Android's standard uninstall dialog)
  • WRITE_SETTINGS — Used to adjust screen brightness and display settings in the Tweaks section

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

HTG TurboX uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

Ad formats used in the App:

  • Rewarded Ads — Optional videos you choose to watch to unlock features or earn tokens
  • Rewarded Interstitial Ads — Full-screen ads shown as a fallback when rewarded ads are unavailable
  • Interstitial Ads — Full-screen ads shown between actions (e.g., before launching a game)

To opt out of personalized ads: Go to your device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information, review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob Privacy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

🪙 Token Reward System

HTG TurboX includes a token-based reward system. Here is how your data is handled:

  • All token balances and earn history are stored only on your device
  • Token rewards are earned by using app features, watching ads, daily logins, and sharing the app
  • Redemption requests (at 10,000 tokens) are submitted voluntarily via your email app and include only your email address and token count
  • We do not track token data on any server — there is no account system

🔔 Notifications

HTG TurboX sends periodic reminder notifications to encourage phone optimization. These notifications:

  • Are scheduled locally using Android WorkManager
  • Do not involve any external server
  • Can be disabled anytime: Settings → Apps → HTG TurboX → Notifications → Turn Off
  • On Android 13+, you will be asked for permission before any notification is sent

🌍 Data Storage & Security

HTG TurboX does not operate any backend servers or databases. All app data (settings, tokens, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage systems (SharedPreferences).

We do not transfer your data internationally. The only data that leaves your device is what Google AdMob collects for advertising purposes, as described above.

🔗 Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising network. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — Required for AdMob and core Android functionality. Privacy Policy
  • AndroidX WorkManager — Used for background notification scheduling. No data collection.

⚖️ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — You can view all locally stored data through your device's App Info screen
  • Deletion — Clear app data anytime via Settings → Apps → HTG TurboX → Clear Data (this resets all tokens and settings)
  • Opt-out of ads — Disable personalized ads via Google Settings on your device
  • Notification control — Disable notifications via Android notification settings
