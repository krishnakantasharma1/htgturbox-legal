Privacy Policy — HTG TurboX

Last updated: March 2026

HTG TurboX ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by Hi Tech Gamerz. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. However, our third-party services may collect data:

  • Google AdMob: Collects device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Device Information: The app reads RAM, battery, and storage data locally on your device. This data is never sent to any server.

Permissions We Use

  • INTERNET: Required to load advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check network connectivity.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Keeps screen on during gaming mode when enabled by user.
  • ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY: Used for Do Not Disturb mode in Game Booster, only when user enables it.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES: Used to display installed apps in App Manager and Game Booster. No app data is shared or stored.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to send optional reminders to clean RAM/junk. Can be disabled in device settings.

Advertising

HTG TurboX uses Google AdMob to display rewarded video ads. By watching ads, users unlock app features. AdMob may use your advertising ID to serve personalized ads. You can opt out via your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of personalized ads.

Data Storage

All data processed by HTG TurboX (RAM stats, battery info, game list) is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers and do not transmit your data anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
